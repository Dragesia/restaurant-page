Restaurant de Cuccittini

Brand chef Argentinian-born maestro Lionel Andres Messi Cuccittini has drawn on over 30 years' experience. Come and visit us today for a great tasting Argentinian food served in the grand atmosphere. Restaurant de Cuccittini blends the energy of Buenos Aires' legendary cantinas with the charm of Europe's open-air markets. This warm, intimate space features the best of Argentinian cuisine.
